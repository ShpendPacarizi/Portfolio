The design of road that connects villages Osek Hile - Madanaj in Gjakova's municipality

This road is located in municipality of Gjakova The road alignment, for most of its length, follows the existing road, except in certain cases where the technical road design standards do not allow it. The terrain through which the designed road alignment passes is hilly. Some of the main parameters of this road are given below:

  • Maximum speed (km/h): 40;
  • Maximum curvature radius (m): 1200;
  • Minimum curvature radius (m): 20
  • Maximum longitudinal slope (%): 9.63
  • Minimum longitudinal slope (%): 0.69
  • Superelevation (%): 2.50

The road construction layers have been adopted as follows:

  • Gravel 50 – 150 mm: t = 30 cm
  • Gravel 0 – 63 mm: t = 20 cm
  • Gravel 0 – 31.5 mm: t = 10 cm
  • Asphalt BA 22 mm: t = 8 cm
  • Asphalt AB 11 mm: t = 4 cm
